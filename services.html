Building a Gaming PC

The first step to becoming a gamer is getting something to game on. This guide will show you how to build your very own Gaming PC. I certainly know how to do this, but teaching how to do it is a whole new thing. In order for me not to get too technical, and to avoid legal problems if you accidentally burn your house down, I think it's best if we leave the teaching to a professional. Here is a helpful video from Jayztwocents showing how to build a PC.

Now, you may watch this video and think, "what the heck? Those parts are super high end, and I can't afford them! How do you expect me to spend one million dollars on my computer? Well, this is where I can help. Building a computer with high end parts is pretty much the same as with mid or low end parts too, just that some high end cases may have more quality-of-life features like better cable management. In order to know what parts to pick for your PC, you first need to establish a budget that you are willing to spend. For some people that could be one million dollars, for others $300, $700, or whatever. Any budget can work. Here's a helpful article for how to choose your parts to match your budget. It may be a little old, so be on the lookout for deals on newer parts that are out, but the main idea stays the same.
